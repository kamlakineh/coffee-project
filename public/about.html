About Us

Who We Are

Saticho trading started from humble beginnings in 2019 by opening a coffee washing station in the small village of chri, in the Sidama region of Ethiopia by a young and visionary entrepreneur named Bedilu Kebede Kayamo. Since 2019, we accumulated a vast experience working with different coffee exporters and aim to grow our reach even further in the coming years to become one of the leading coffee exporters in the country.
