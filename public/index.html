who we are

Our Beautiful Coffee Trading PLC

Saticho trading started from humble beginnings in 2019 by opening a coffee washing station in the small village of chri, in the Sidama region of Ethiopia by a young and visionary entrepreneur named Bedilu Kebede Kayamo. Since 2019, we accumulated a vast experience working with different coffee exporters and aim to grow our reach even further in the coming years to become one of the leading coffee exporters in the country.

Natural

Natural coffee, also known as dry-processed coffee, is a method of coffee bean processing that differs from the washed process.

Washed

Washed coffee, also known as wet-processed coffee, is a method of coffee bean processing that involves several key steps to produce high-quality coffee.

Honey

Honey process coffee involves removing a bean's outer layer before letting it dry

Anaerobic

A coffee that was fermented in an environment that lacks oxygen..

Roasted

Roasted coffee is the result of a meticulous process that transforms raw coffee beans into the flavorful and aromatic product we enjoy, involving key steps to ensure a high-quality cup.

What types of coffee processing methods does Saticho Coffee Export utilize for its beans?

>
Saticho Coffee Export employs both natural (dry-processed) and washed (wet-processed) coffee processing methods for its beans. This allows us to offer a diverse range of coffees with distinct flavor profiles, from the fruity and full-bodied notes of natural processed coffees to the bright acidity and clean taste of washed processed coffees.

What sustainability practices does Saticho Coffee Export implement in its operations?

>
Saticho Coffee Export is committed to sustainable and ethical practices in coffee production. We support environmentally friendly farming methods that promote soil health, water conservation, and biodiversity. Our partnerships with local farmers emphasize fair labor practices and community development initiatives. Furthermore, we prioritize sustainable packaging and shipping practices to minimize our environmental footprint.

What regions in Ethiopia does Saticho Coffee Export source its coffee beans from?

>
Saticho Coffee Export sources its coffee beans from various renowned coffee-growing regions in Ethiopia, including but not limited to Sidama, Guji and Yirgacheffe. Each region offers unique flavor profiles and characteristics, contributing to the diverse range of high-quality coffees that we export to our clients worldwide.
